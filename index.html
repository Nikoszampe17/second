About Me

Nikos Zampetakis is a physiotherapist holding a degree from the University of West Attica. His patient-centered approach focuses on thoroughly understanding the root cause of musculoskeletal issues to maximize treatment effectiveness. By applying key musculoskeletal physiotherapy principles in assessment, Nikos collaborates with each individual to develop a personalized treatment plan aimed at effectively managing pain and restoring mobility.
